  • Press spacebar to roll the dice.
  • Each player moves forward that many tiles.
  • Land on a star to collect one.
  • Step on a tile once: it turns gray.
  • Step on the same tile twice: it turns black (cracked).
  • Landing on a cracked tile sends you back to the start.
  • First to the target number of stars wins!
